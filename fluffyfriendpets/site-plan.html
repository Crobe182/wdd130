Overview

Purpose

To help inform people why it is a good idea to adopt a pet, especially from a shelter, and to help them find shelters near them. To lower the number of pets in shelters by persuading poeple to adopt from shelters.

Audience

Those who are looking to brighten up their life or mix things up a bit by finding a pet to adopt.

Content

Home page

Welcome to Fluffy Friends, your ultimate resource for adopting pets from your local animal shelter. Our mission is to connect loving individuals and families with pets in need of a forever home. We provide comprehensive information, practical advice, and an easy-to-navigate platform to make the adoption process smooth and rewarding.

Images for the Home page

Adoption Process

Detailed guides and FAQs help users understand the steps involved in adopting a pet, from initial inquiry to bringing the pet home. These resources cover everything from completing an application to finalizing the adoption, ensuring adopters know what to expect.

Images for the Page 2

Resources for Adopters

Articles and checklists on pet-proofing the home, gathering supplies, and preparing for the first days with a new pet ensure a smooth transition. These resources address common concerns and challenges during the initial adjustment period.
